How to Request Data Deletion

You have two options to request deletion of your data:

Option 1: Through Facebook (Recommended)

If you connected your Facebook account, you can request data deletion directly through Facebook:

  1. Go to your Facebook App Settings
  2. Find "LiqAmz Inventory System" in your connected apps
  3. Click "Remove" or "Delete Data"
  4. Facebook will automatically send a deletion request to our system

Our system will process the deletion request within 30 days.

Option 2: Direct Request

You can request data deletion by contacting us directly:

  1. Send an email to odai.alkhatib@icloud.com
  2. Include the subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  3. Provide your account email address or user ID
  4. Specify if you want to delete all data or just Facebook integration data

We will confirm receipt of your request within 48 hours and complete deletion within 30 days.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request data deletion, we will remove:

  • Account Information: Your email, name, and account settings
  • Product Data: All products, inventory records, and product images
  • Facebook Integration: All Facebook access tokens, page connections, and catalog data
  • Scan History: All barcode scan records and history
  • User Preferences: All saved settings and preferences

What Data May Be Retained

For legal and legitimate business purposes, we may retain certain data:

  • Transaction records required by law (e.g., payment records)
  • Anonymized analytics data (cannot be linked to you)
  • Data required for legal compliance or dispute resolution

This retained data will be kept for the minimum period required by law.

Facebook-Specific Data Deletion

If you only want to delete your Facebook integration data:

  1. Log into your account
  2. Go to Settings → Facebook Integration
  3. Click "Disconnect Facebook Account"
  4. Confirm the disconnection

This will immediately remove your Facebook access tokens and page connections, but will keep your account and other data.
