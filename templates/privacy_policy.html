1. Introduction

LiqAmz Inventory System ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our inventory management application and Facebook integration services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and other account details
  • Product Data: Product information, barcodes, inventory data, and product images
  • Facebook Integration: When you connect your Facebook account, we store encrypted access tokens to enable posting products to your Facebook Page and Shop

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage data and analytics
  • Device information and IP address
  • Log data from API requests

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our inventory management services
  • Enable Facebook integration features (posting products to your Facebook Page/Shop)
  • Process transactions and manage your account
  • Send you technical notices and support messages
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Facebook Integration

When you connect your Facebook account, we request the following permissions:

  • pages_show_list: To list the Facebook Pages you manage
  • pages_read_engagement: To read engagement data from your pages
  • pages_manage_posts: To post products to your Facebook Page

We store your Facebook access tokens in encrypted form and use them solely to:

  • Post products to your selected Facebook Page
  • Create products in your Facebook Shop catalog
  • Manage your Facebook Page posts

We do not share your Facebook data with third parties except as necessary to provide these services.

5. Data Storage and Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Data encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encrypted storage of sensitive tokens
  • Secure database hosting with Supabase
  • Regular security audits and updates

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our services (e.g., Supabase for database hosting, Facebook for integration features)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (see Data Deletion section below)
  • Disconnect Facebook: Disconnect your Facebook integration at any time through the app settings
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

8. Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data, including Facebook integration data, at any time. To request data deletion:

  • Visit our Data Deletion Instructions page
  • Or contact us directly at the email address below

When you request data deletion, we will:

  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Remove your Facebook integration and encrypted tokens
  • Delete all product and inventory data
  • Complete deletion within 30 days of your request

Note: Some data may be retained for legal or legitimate business purposes (e.g., transaction records required by law).

9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our service and hold certain information. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

10. Children's Privacy
